Probing the Dark Side of the Universe

I am an broadly interested in the most fundamental questions in extragalactic astronomy and cosmology, in particular the "dark side" of the universe: black holes, dark matter, and dark energy. As an observer, I use multi-wavelength observations (from UV to radio) to investigate open questions in these fields. I also use strong gravitational lensing as a natural telescope, which boosts the sensitivity and spatial resolution of observations.
