--- layout: research title: Minghao Yue's Research ---

I am broadly interested in many subfields in extragalactic astronomy and cosmology, with a focus on high-redshift quasars and strong gravitational lensing. As an observer, I use both large survey datasets and multi-wavelength observations to investigate open questions in these fields.

My PhD thesis is a survey for lensed quasars at z>~5, which is the first systematic survey for such objects. Click here to check the mock lensed quasars and simulated observations I made for this survey. See projects below for some of our exciting discoveries!

I am working on in-depth studies of the most distant gravitationally lensed quasar (J0439+1634 at z=6.52).

We discovered the most distant kpc-scale quasar pair (J2037-4537 at z=5.66).

I use strongly lensed systems to constrain cosmology parameters.
